Ingredients:

Citrus:

Some examples of great starting ingredients in the citrus category:
Bergamot FCF
Orange oil
Lemon oil
Optional: Aldehyde C10

Aquatic/marine:

Helional
Optional extension: Calone
Dihydromyrcenol
Ambroxan (ambrofix, ambrox, ambermol dl, ambroxide)

Traditional:

Labdanum absolut (resinoid)
Veramoss (oakmoss)
Amyl salicylate (clovelike)
Lavender EO (top-mid)
Patchouli EO

Musks:

*Ethylene brassylate (clean anamalic musk soft)
Galaxolide - (bad for env, non-biodegradable)
Romandolide - similar to galaxolide but less bad
Makes perfumes last longer Pick one: Habanolide - clean hot iron musk modern
Muscenone - powdery and strong anamalic
Ambrettolide - sweet powdery
Edenolide- top note, clean sweeter fruity fresh, shorter last

Sweet / Gourmand:

Vanillin - sweet,
Ethyl maltol - caramel toffee radiant silage
Coumarin - fuger accord, sweeter, marzipan
Optional Veratraldehyde - fruit sweet cherry
Heliotropin DEA - cherry freit floral

Woody:

ISO E super - moody cedar radiant velvet
Vertofix - cedar
Optional extension - cedarwood Virginian (clean)
Pick one: Eugenol - spicy common cinnamon clove warm
Vetiver EO - darker, rooty, trad, warm
Bacdanol / sandalwood (good quality) - clean modern sandal alternative

Floral:

Hedione - sweet floral (jasmine accord) super versatile
Rose base (cheaper and good) / EO / absolute
Aurantiol or Neroli EO
Methyl ionone gamma (or other ionone like beta ionone)
Lily florol molecule fresh and radiant without much else
Jasmine absolute (expensive)
Fruity / tropical: Anisaldehyde - mint (long last) heliotrope leaning, sweeter floral fruity, not overbearing
Aldehyde c18 (coconut) or c14 (peach) tonic effect soft and creamy
Cassis Base 345b - black currant, passionfruit, mango, guava, blend
